## Upgrade Note – Instructional Video

This video shows how to upgrade a Note to a newer version in Lyric Studio.

Click on this [video link](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1zuW_noEZMCPAX00QQNDiHX37uCz2XHx8/view?usp=sharing>) to view the note upgrade instructional video.

## Approach to Video Creation and Scripting

The instructional video was created using a structured approach that focused on clarity, pacing, and user understanding. The goal was to simulate a realistic product walkthrough while working within the limited set of available screenshots.

## Planning the video

The process began by reviewing the provided steps for upgrading a Note and identifying the actions that needed to be shown. Since only a small number of screenshots were available, the video was planned to include:

- a short introduction to set context
- step-by-step narration of the workflow
- emphasis on important decision points, such as version selection
- a short closing segment to reinforce the outcome

A script was created to ensure that the narration stayed aligned with the provided steps and did not introduce unsupported assumptions.

## Creating the visual flow

To maintain engagement and reach the required duration, supporting visual elements were added around the product screenshots.

- **Canva** was used to create the introduction and closing slides
- stock visuals were used to provide a smooth opening and ending
- screenshots were sequenced to reflect the actual user flow

This helped create a complete viewing experience while staying aligned with the feature steps.

## Recording and editing

The video was assembled and refined using editing tools that supported timing, sequencing, and transitions.

- **Camtasia** was used to structure the timeline, place screenshots, and manage the overall flow
- **Microsoft Clipchamp** was used to refine playback timing and improve the final pacing
- transitions and zoom effects were used to guide attention to important areas on the screen

## Voiceover creation

The narration was developed to make the feature flow easy to follow.

- the voiceover script was prepared based on the provided workflow
- **Blaster Suite** was used to generate the voiceover
- pacing and emphasis were adjusted so each step could be understood clearly

This helped create a consistent voice and avoid rushed explanations.

## Image refinement

The screenshots were updated where needed to improve clarity and focus.

- **Snagit** was used to edit screenshots
- key areas were highlighted where necessary
- distracting or unnecessary visual elements were reduced

This made the screenshots easier to follow during the video.

## Final refinement

The final video was reviewed to make sure that:

- the narration matched the screenshots
- the sequence followed the actual feature flow
- the pacing felt steady and clear
- the visuals were readable and easy to understand

The result is a short instructional video that combines screenshots, supporting visuals, narration, and editing to explain the Note Upgrade process in a clear and structured way.
